THE BEGINNING

It Started With a Prayer
That Changed Everything

"We didn't have a building. We didn't have a budget. We didn't even have a plan. All we had was a living room, twelve broken people, and a God who specializes in impossible beginnings."

In the spring of 2018, Pastor gathered a handful of strangers in his home. They were teachers, mechanics, students, and single mothers — none of them qualified by the world's standards, all of them hungry for something real.

That first night, they prayed until the sun came up. No one wanted to leave. What happened in that living room was not religious. It was raw. Honest. Desperate. People who had given up on church found themselves weeping in the presence of God. Marriages on the verge of collapse found hope. A man who hadn't spoken to his father in ten years picked up the phone that night.

ARC — The Ark of God — was never meant to be just another church. It was born to be a refuge for the broken, a launchpad for the called, and a home for everyone in between.

Foundation of Faith

What We Believe

Our beliefs are not just theology — they are the lived experience of a community that has seen God move.

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and the final authority for faith and life. Scripture reveals God’s character, His will, and His redemptive plan for humanity.

We believe in one God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. He is the Creator of all things and is perfect in holiness, love, wisdom, and power.

We believe in the deity of Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, sinless life, atoning death on the cross, bodily resurrection, and His coming return in power and glory.

We believe salvation is a gift of grace received through faith in Jesus Christ alone. It is not earned by works, but freely given through repentance and belief.

We believe the Holy Spirit is fully God and active today—empowering believers, guiding them into truth, and equipping the Church with spiritual gifts.

We believe the Church is the Body of Christ, called to worship God, make disciples, live in community, and demonstrate God’s love to the world.

We believe God still heals and performs miracles today through the power of the Holy Spirit, bringing restoration and breakthrough according to His will.

We believe in the resurrection of the saved and the lost—eternal life with God for those who believe in Christ, and eternal separation for those who reject Him.
