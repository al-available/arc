WELCOME HOME

More Than a Church.
A Family.

At ARC, we believe the church is not a building but a living, breathing community of believers united by faith, love, and a shared purpose. Like Noah’s Ark, we are a place of refuge in the storms of life — a sanctuary where hope is renewed and lives are transformed.

Whether you are taking your first steps in faith or have walked with God for decades, there is a place for you here. Come as you are. Leave changed.
